## CRAT Analysis

A Charitable Remainder Annuity Trust (CRAT) is a trust structure that provides a fixed annuity to one or more noncharitable beneficiaries, with the remainder interest passing to a designated charity. CRATs can be structured for the lifetime of the beneficiary or for a term of years, with a maximum term of 20 years as specified under 26 CFR §1.664-2 [S004].
